About Me

Hello! I'm Inoo Jo, a third-year mechanical engineering student at The University of Texas at Austin with a passion for robotics, mechanical design, and mathematical analysis. I work as a student technician at Texas Inventionworks—a state-of-the-art makerspace and prototyping facility—where I train students on machine tools and guide them through the manufacturing process for class, research, and personal projects.

I'm also a member of the UT Climbing Team and enjoy applying my engineering skills to create climbing-specific tools and training equipment. In my free time, you can usually find me working on a side project or at the climbing gym. I love picking up new skills—one of the main reasons I built this portfolio site. Feel free to check out the Contact Me section or send me a message!
